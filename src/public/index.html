About us

  • Who We Are?

    We are a team of highly professional and educated individuals that include qualified pharmacists, business developer's, customer's relation officers, protocol officers, managers, and administrators. Our team has efficiently served in top retail pharmacy chains of Pakistan & abroad for quite a long time and has played a vital role in the development of modern health care system in society.

  • Why Are We Here?

    Due to lack of modern pharmacies in Southern Punjab, people are getting unregistered, fake, substandard, and harmful medicine. To serve the nation and help the patient in need, we plan to launch state of the art of new pharmacies in Southern Punjab. We plan to spread out a network of new pharmacies to the remote and far-flung areas of Southern Punjab where people will be able to get quality medicine with 100% efficacy.

  • What We Do?

    We are spreading a network of network of new pharmacies in Southern Punjab with a vision and ambition of providing 100% quality medicine directly purchased from an authorized distributor of Pharma Companies. Our establishments are solar powered to ensure continuous power supply to maintain the desired temperature in the medicine hall and fridge.

  • How Are We Better?

    Our professional team and qualified pharmacists work hard to ensure quality, quantity, and efficacy of the medicine. For this cause, we ensure that medication is kept under controlled temperature away from sunlight and humidity. For this purpose, we have installed state of the art solar system at our pharmacies that ensure 24 Hours power supply to cooling units. Out fridges work 24 Hours to keep hormones, insulin, vaccinations, interferons and other fridge items from 2 to 8 degree under controlled temperature. Our pharmacists keep track of the medicine's expiry on daily bases and remove the medication from shelves six months before the expiry date.
